About Us

A Family-Friendly Spot in Himayatnagar

Tasty Route is your go-to pure vegetarian restaurant nestled in the heart of Himayatnagar, Hyderabad — conveniently close to Page Junior College. We believe great food should be fresh, fast, and full of flavour.

From hearty breakfast bites to indulgent waffles and refreshing beverages, our menu is crafted to satisfy every craving. Whether you're stopping in after college, bringing the family, or just need a quick, delicious meal — we've got you covered.
